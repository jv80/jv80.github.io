Locating bus stops in Kamloops,BC

An interprise GIS that show location and Information about bus stops in Kamloops,BC

Locating bus stops in the city of Kamloops, BC is a multi-user application enterprise GIS that shows location and information of the bus stops of the city of Kamloops. I developed this project using Free and Open Source Software for Geospatial (FOSS4G).

For this project, I used a total of three shape data file from The City of Kamloops Open Data Catalog.
